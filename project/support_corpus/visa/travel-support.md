## Extracted support content

- Visa’s support page says travellers can use the travel-support information for common questions about ATM availability, emergency services, and how to get help while abroad.
- The page also says travel-notification questions should be handled by referring to the card issuer’s site.
- For damaged, lost, stolen, or compromised cards while travelling, Visa says it can work with the financial institution to approve and expedite emergency card delivery, usually within one to three days.
- The same page includes ATM-locator guidance and consumer guidance for 3-D Secure issues, which generally routes users back to their issuer or bank for account-specific support.
