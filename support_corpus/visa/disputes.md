# Visa Disputes

Source site: https://www.visa.co.in/support.html
Company: Visa
Topic: Disputed charges and merchant-related concerns

## Suggested source section(s)

- How do I dispute a charge?
- Merchant concerns

## Extracted support content

- Visa’s support page says disputed charges should be handled by contacting the card issuer or bank using the phone number on the card.
- The page says issuers often require detailed transaction information before resolving a dispute.
- For merchant-related concerns, Visa’s support page says users can take action immediately through the merchant concern form linked from the support page.
- The page also notes that Visa does not directly set up, service, or access cardholder or merchant accounts; those are handled by financial institutions.
- This source supports safe replies that direct users toward their issuer/bank for disputes and merchant-account issues.
